# Senior React Developer – Behavioral & Managerial Interview Questions

A structured list of behavioral and managerial interview questions commonly asked for **Senior / Lead React Developers (7+ years experience)**.

These questions focus on **leadership, ownership, collaboration, delivery, and professional growth**.

---

# 1. Introduction & Background

1. Can you briefly introduce yourself?
2. Can you walk us through one of your recent projects?
3. What were your roles and responsibilities in that project?
4. How large was your team, and what roles were involved?
5. Which Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) methodology did your team follow (Agile, Scrum, Kanban, etc.)?
6. What type of product or domain was your project focused on?

---

# 2. Ownership & Responsibility

1. What is the most significant task you have accomplished in your career so far?
2. Why do you consider it significant?
3. Tell us about a time when you took ownership of a critical feature or project.
4. Have you ever taken responsibility for a problem that was outside your assigned role?
5. How do you ensure accountability for the work you deliver?

---

# 3. Problem Solving & Challenging Situations

1. What is the most challenging project you have worked on so far?
2. Why was it challenging?
3. What was the most difficult technical problem you have solved in your career?
4. What approach did you follow to solve that problem?
5. What did you learn from that experience?
6. Describe a complex problem you faced and how you broke it down to solve it.
7. Tell me about a time when you had to make a difficult technical decision.

---

# 4. Mistakes & Learning

1. Tell me about a time when you made a mistake at work.
2. What went wrong in that situation?
3. How did you handle it?
4. What did you learn from that experience?
5. How did you ensure the same mistake did not happen again?

---

# 5. Conflict Resolution & Team Collaboration

1. Tell me about a situation where you had a conflict with a team member.
2. What caused the conflict?
3. How did you handle the situation?
4. What was the final outcome?
5. How do you handle disagreements during technical discussions?
6. How do you handle situations where teammates strongly disagree with your solution?
7. How do you ensure healthy collaboration within your team?

---

# 6. Communication & Stakeholder Management

1. How do you communicate technical challenges to non-technical stakeholders?
2. How do you keep stakeholders informed about project progress?
3. Tell me about a time when you had to explain a complex technical concept to someone without a technical background.
4. How do you handle situations where business requirements are unclear?

---

# 7. Delivery & Deadlines

1. Have you ever missed a deadline?
2. What caused the delay?
3. How did you handle the situation with your team and stakeholders?
4. If you realize that certain features cannot be completed within the given timeline, what would be your immediate actions?
5. How would you communicate the risk of missing a deadline to stakeholders?
6. What steps would you take to prevent such situations in the future?
7. How do you manage multiple tasks with competing deadlines?

---

# 8. Work Ethic & Professional Attitude

1. If your manager asks you to occasionally work on weekends to meet critical deadlines, how would you respond?
2. How do you maintain productivity during high-pressure situations?
3. How do you maintain work-life balance while handling demanding projects?

---

# 9. Leadership & Mentoring

1. Have you ever mentored junior developers?
2. How do you help junior engineers grow technically?
3. How do you conduct code reviews effectively?
4. How do you ensure coding standards are maintained across the team?
5. Tell me about a time when you helped a team member overcome a technical challenge.
6. How do you lead technical discussions within your team?

---

# 10. Feedback & Self Awareness

1. If I asked your manager to describe something positive about you, what would they say?
2. If I asked your manager about an area where you could improve, what would they say?
3. What are your biggest strengths as a developer?
4. What are some areas where you believe you still need improvement?
5. How do you actively work on improving your skills?

---

# 11. Motivation & Career Growth

1. What motivates you in your day-to-day work?
2. What keeps you engaged and excited about software development?
3. How do you keep yourself updated with new technologies?
4. Where do you see yourself in the next few years?
5. What kind of technical challenges excite you the most?

---

# 12. Team & Organizational Impact

1. How do you contribute to improving team productivity?
2. Have you ever introduced a new process or improvement in your team?
3. How do you handle situations where the team is underperforming?
4. How do you encourage knowledge sharing within the team?
5. What steps do you take to improve overall team efficiency?
